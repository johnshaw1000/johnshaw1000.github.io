Hanbury and Draycott Bowls Club

Crown Green bowls club in the village of Hanbury, Staffordshire, England

Teams

The Hanbury & Draycott Bowls Club is in the village of Hanbury, Staffordshire next to the Memorial Hall on Anslow Road. The Club is a member of the Crown Green Bowling Association. We have three teams in league & cup competitions:

  • Veterans (over 50s) - Needwood League Division 3 - Monday afternoons
  • Ladies - Burton League Divison 3 - Tuesday evenings
  • Mixed (all ages and genders) - Burton Mid Week League Division 1 - Wednesday evenings

Club nights are on Thursday Evenings at 7pm and everyone is welcome. Refreshments provided. New members are always very welcome.

The outdoor season runs from April to September. There is an indoor section too which runs in the off-season, please contact us for further details.
